  • Some of the process improvements I implemented include:

    • Introduction of a new system of packing where labels are printed on each packing desk as an order completes. This required system changes but has increased efficiency and accuracy of order processing.

    • Identification and implementation of a streamlined warehouse air mail process, removing the need to handle and sort 90% of the shipments which now saves the business 40 man hours per week (representing an £18,000 annual efficiency saving).
